# Pitch Doc: B R I Q U E

**Vision:**
**B R I Q U E** elevates a mundane red clay block into a serialized, high-end design statement. Inspired by minimal hardware houses like Apple and luxury brands like Balenciaga, our vision was to create a gorgeous obsidian landing page that treats a brick as a Veblen good. We implemented an interactive 3D hero showcase (Three.js), scroll-reveal transitions, and "B R I Q U E   S M A S H"—a physics slingshot game simulating planned obsolescence, where users fling an appreciating brick (+$1.5/s) to rescue depreciating smart devices and burst volatile market bubbles before they crash.

**Tools & Prompting Style:**
We leveraged AI image generators for luxury lifestyle mockups, React for modular state, and a clean, responsive CSS design. We used declarative and precise technical prompts to iterate on Three.js material shaders and canvas physics to achieve fluid 60FPS performance.

**Satisfaction:**
We are extremely satisfied; it perfectly balances premium UI aesthetics, engaging game mechanics, and deep satirical humor, keeping attention spans far beyond 3 seconds.
