Welcome to the digital showroom of **B R I Q U E**, the world's premier unapologetically analogue monolith. 

## The Product
B R I Q U E is a bespoke, hand-glazed 3.2-kilogram block of pure red clay fired at 2,000°F. In an era dominated by transient digital notifications, planned software obsolescence, and battery degradation, B R I Q U E offers a permanent anchor. It is the ultimate veblen-grade status piece to ground your office, penthouse, or yacht.

## Core Features
* **Zero Digital Overhead:** No Bluetooth, no Wi-Fi, no location tracking, and absolutely no software updates.
* **Resonant Telemetry:** Dropping it on solid concrete yields a heavy, deep 102 Hz thud of absolute structural presence.
* **Swiss-Ledger Registry:** Every single piece is kiln-embossed with a unique serial number and registered in our vault archives.
